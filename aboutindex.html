Movie of the Week

Coolie (2025 Tamil film) Director: Lokesh Kanagaraj Stars: Rajinikanth, Nagarjuna Akkineni, Soubin Shahir, Upendra, Shruti Haasan, Sathyaraj, and Rachita Ram, with special appearances by Aamir Khan and Pooja Hegde. Plot: Deva (Rajinikanth) investigates the suspicious death of his friend and gets entangled in a gold smuggling syndicate. This leads him to confront his own past as a former coolie union leader who fought against exploitation. Release Date: August 14, 2025. Reception: Mixed-to-positive reviews from critics, but praised by fans for performances, action, music, and the first half. It became the highest-grossing Tamil film of 2025 and is eyeing the ₹600 crore club. Interesting facts: Marks Nagarjuna's first-ever antagonist role. Aamir Khan agreed to his cameo without reading the script. Rajinikanth received an "A" certificate after 36 years. The film is rumored to be the first Indian film shot with IMAX-certified cameras.
